1. Changing many statuses at one shoot
	Select status controler cells. Press space to edit status of last selected controler. 
	(Pressing space will not deselect other cells). Choosing one of the statuses will change statuses
	of all other selected controllers.
	
2. Switching fixed to free or free to fixed with one click
	To switch parameter status from fixed (either local or common) to free and vice versa click the status
	selector (combo box at the top of parameters grid) with middle mouse button.
	
3. Switching local to common or common to local with one click
	To switch parameter status from local (either free or fixed) to common and vice versa hold
	keyboard control key and click the status selector 
	(combo box at the top of parameters grid) with middle mouse button.
